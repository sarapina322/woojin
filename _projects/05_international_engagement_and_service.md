I value work that is collaborative, international, and directly useful to people. During my undergraduate years, I participated in several programs that shaped this perspective.

### Representative experiences

- **Korea University International Student Assistant (KUISA)** buddy assistant
- operations staff for the **26th ANOC General Assembly Seoul 2022**
- delegate at the **2023 ICM Model UNESCO Conference**
- volunteer in the **Sports Talent Sharing Program for Vulnerable Groups**

### Why this matters to me

These roles strengthened my ability to work across cultures, explain things clearly, and support people in unfamiliar environments. That same mindset continues to shape how I think about healthcare AI: the system has to make sense to the human being using it.
