Before graduate research became the center of my work, a large part of my undergraduate life was built around **student leadership**, **sports communication**, and **industry-facing experience**.

### Representative experiences

- student council member, class representative, and student council president in the Department of Physical Education
- student reporter / journalist for sports-related campus organizations
- English assistant of athletes in international or multilingual settings
- internships with the **KB Insurance Volleyball Team** and the **Korean Rugby Association**

These experiences trained me to communicate clearly, organize people and events, and understand how teams actually operate beyond theory.
